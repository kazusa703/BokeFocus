How to Use

  1. Choose a photo from your library
  2. Tap on the object you want to keep sharp, or draw a box around it
  3. Add points to refine the selection (green = include, red = exclude)
  4. Adjust the blur intensity with the slider
  5. Tap Next to fine-tune with the brush tool
  6. Save the result to your photo library

FAQ

Q: Why is the selection not accurate?
A: Try drawing a tighter bounding box around the object, or add positive/negative points to refine the edges.

Q: Can I undo my edits?
A: Yes, use the undo button (arrow icon) in the top-left corner.

Q: Where are saved photos stored?
A: Edited photos are saved to your device's photo library.
