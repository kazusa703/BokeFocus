Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Overview

BokeFocus ("the App") is a background blur photo editor developed by an individual developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All image processing is performed entirely on your device.

Photos

The App accesses your photo library only when you explicitly select a photo to edit. Photos are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server. Edited photos are saved to your photo library only when you tap the Save button.

Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings or by declining the App Tracking Transparency prompt.

For more information on Google's data practices, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

The App will ask for your permission before accessing your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). This identifier is used solely for displaying relevant advertisements. You may decline this request, and the App will continue to function normally with non-personalized ads.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional in-app purchase to remove advertisements. Purchase transactions are handled securely by Apple and are not processed by the developer.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. No other third-party services collect personal information.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Support page.
